## Overview

AI music generation tools use deep learning to create original music from text prompts, melodic inputs, or style references. These systems can produce full tracks with vocals, instruments, and production, often in seconds. They lower the barrier for music creation while raising questions about authorship, originality, and the future of professional music production.

## Topics in This Section

### [[suno|Suno]]
A text-to-music platform capable of generating complete songs with vocals and instrumentation from a text prompt. Suno produces surprisingly coherent lyrics, melodies, and production across many genres.

### [[udio|Udio]]
A music generation tool focused on high-quality audio output with detailed genre and style control. Udio allows iterative editing and extension of generated clips.

## Key Concepts

- **Text-to-Music**: Generating audio directly from natural language descriptions of style, mood, genre, and lyrics
- **Continuation**: Extending an existing audio clip while maintaining musical coherence
- **Style Transfer**: Applying the sonic characteristics of one piece of music to another
- **Stem Separation**: Isolating individual instrument tracks from a mixed recording (enables AI remixing)
- **MIDI Generation**: Creating note-level music data that can be edited in a DAW rather than rendering audio directly
