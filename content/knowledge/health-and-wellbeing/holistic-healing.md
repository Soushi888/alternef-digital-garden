Holistic healing encompasses therapeutic approaches that treat the whole person rather than specific symptoms. It recognizes that physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual dimensions are interconnected — and that lasting healing requires addressing all of them.

## Approaches

- **Traditional Chinese Medicine**: Balancing qi through acupuncture, herbs, and movement
- **Ayurveda**: Constitutional-based medicine from the Indian tradition
- **Essenian Healing**: Somatic and energetic healing traditions
- **Mind-body medicine**: Practices that work the mind-body connection (yoga, qi gong, meditation)
